About us

h4 Where it all started

Our story started with a problem (the best ideas usually do). Some ten years ago, our founder was furnishing his flat. Frustrated at the lack of well-designed, good quality and affordable sofas, he set about redefining the process.

Everything at Nordic House has been sourced from or created in collaboration with artisans and talented makers in Scandinavia, the UK and Europe. We’ve been pioneering Scandi-inspired living and the hygge way of life since 2009, and we’re passionate about slow living. For us, it’s not just about the aesthetic but the meaning behind it.
